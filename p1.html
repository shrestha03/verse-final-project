Evaluation of Indic Language Embeddings for Content Enrichment

This project aims to evaluate the effectiveness of Indic language embeddings for content enrichment. We will analyze the impact of using these embeddings on various natural language processing tasks such as sentiment analysis, text classification, and named entity recognition. The evaluation will involve comparing the performance of different embedding models on a benchmark dataset. The findings of this study will contribute to enhancing the understanding and usage of Indic language embeddings in the field of NLP.
